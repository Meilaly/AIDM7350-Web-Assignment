Some Facts About DaiDai

（○｀ 3′○）

DaiDai is a cotton doll bought in my first college year. With its adorable and charming appearance, as well as its affordable price of only 45 yuan, it successfully captured the my heart. From then on, this cute little boy has made a fortune by relying on his outlook and even achieved "financial independence".(At the cost of emptying my wallet)

The reason I bought DaiDai was because an idol. Even though I'm no longer a fandom member anymore, I still keep DaiDai as a baby to take care without fandom attribute.
I used my imagination to create new characters and identities for him.

In my eyes, DaiDai has a very captivating appearance that is perfect for being the protagonist of fairy tales and fantasy stories. I let my imagination run wild and created a completely new persona and identity for Daidai. Just like a Disney princess, he has a collection of beautiful and stunning outfits,(even a lot of princess dresses) as well as his own unique personality and little quirks.

Now it is the fifth year that DaiDai has been with me.
We have been visited many places together. He is not only my best partner, but also a constant source of inspiration for me in writing and painting. I really hope that one day I can use what I've learned about artificial intelligence and digital media to create his complete story.

Birth

Cotton dolls like DaiDai began to appear on the Taobao online marketplace around 2019. Initially, they were only a few dolls designed with the outlook of celebrities. At that time, cotton dolls were not as popular as they are today. I happened to purchase DaiDai during the early stage of the cotton doll trend, which allowed me to witness the growth and development of the cotton doll community firsthand. I hopes in the future the cotton doll industry can be combined with artificial intelligence so that DaiDai can speak and interact in real world.

Development

With the increasing variety of cotton dolls and a growing audience, the types of dolls and doll clothing have gradually expanded from solely featuring celebrity-themed dolls and celebrity-inspired outfits. They have now expanded to include independently created dolls and doll clothing without specific attributes, sold by doll enthusiasts who are interested in the hobby. Some well-known IP brands have also started to enter the market.Various masters in the field are engaging in fierce competition, each launching their own beautifully exquisite cotton doll clothing.

Current

After a period of vibrant growth lasting one to two years, the cotton doll market has gradually matured. Several well-known doll clothing brands have emerged, and on Weibo, there are popular hashtags dedicated to daily updates of the latest doll and doll clothing styles. Major e-commerce platforms such as Xianyu, Taobao, and Weidian have stores selling doll accessories, with Weidian being particularly prominent. Recently, the rising platform Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) is also gradually expanding into the cotton doll market.
