AboutDaiDai

Name: 呆呆

EnglishName: DaiDai

Nickname: Dully,DuDu

(Unlike most protagonists with all their stats maxed out or exceptional growth abilities, DaiDai can be described as "looking not very smart.")

Personality: Dumbfounded, innocent, kind, naive, slow to react, a curious little goofball who is always curious about everything. He often can't figure out situation and sometimes unintentionally get into trouble without realizing it. Fortunately, his mistakes are always forgiven and accepted.
(I hope that DaiDai conveys a theme of love and healing, where mistakes are forgiven regardless of their nature, and it doesn't matter if one is not very smart or capable. The innocent and naive nature of DaiDai's character also aligns with the adorable appearance of a cotton doll.)

Favorite clothing: Various beautiful princess dresses.
(Almost all of my friends say that DaiDai looks even cuter when wearing a little dress.I am also willing to dress him up as "DaiDai Princess" and visit DisneyLand. )

Friend pet: A pink sleepy owl named "Kunkun"(困困) who is always dozing off.
